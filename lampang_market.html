Kad Kong Ta Market

About Kad Kong Ta Market

Kad Kong Ta Market is a historic riverside market along the Wang River, with a history of over 100 years. It is a well-known center for local food, handicrafts, and souvenirs from Lampang.

The market preserves its vintage charm with traditional wooden buildings, old shops, and authentic riverside community lifestyles. It is a popular destination for tourists and photography lovers.
